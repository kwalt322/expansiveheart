Rates

Fees

Cost of therapy sessions are:

  • $120 for 50-minutes
  • $150 for 75-minutes
  • $180 for 90-minute Couples sessions

Insurance

Fees must be paid at time of session and cannot be billed directly to your insurance company. I can provide monthly statements for out-of-network insurance reimbursement, if applicable. Please contact your insurance company regarding out-of-network coverage verification for psychotherapy services with a Marriage and Family Therapist Intern (MFTI). I would be happy to discuss the pros and cons of using insurance, over the phone.

Cancellation Policy

Please notify me of any schedule changes as soon as possible, either in-person, by phone or e-mail.

Any sessions cancelled within 48 hours of the appointment time will be charged the full fee.
